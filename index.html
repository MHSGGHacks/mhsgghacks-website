Hacking with a twist | December 9th, 2017
Frequently Asked Questions
What's a hackathon? A hackathon is an event where people of all skill levels can come together to create a project and develop computer science skills. Any student can come to a hackathon to see how their interests meet technology and collaborate with other enthusiastic students to build an innovative and exciting project.
Wait... what makes GGHacks so special? GGHacks is a special type of hackathon. While other hackathons are focused strictly on serious projects, GGHacks encourages creativity and non-traditional thinking by rewarding humor and less serious entries. GGHacks is a place to let loose and have some fun with like-minded hackers.
Who can attend? Any high school student is welcome to apply! If you're in middle school, you may attend if you are at least 13 years old. Absolutely no coding experience is necessary.
How much does it cost? Nothing at all! GGHacks is completely free to all participants. While you're here, you'll get free food and free stuff.
Will you provide travel reimbursement? Sorry, we're currently unable to provide travel reimbursement.
How long will the event last? GGHacks will last Saturday morning through Sunday afternoon. The hacking period will be 24 hours.
I'm a parent concerned about safety. Is GGHacks safe? Yes! GGHacks is definitely safe. All 24 hours, there are adult chaperones on site monitoring the event around-the-clock. Along with that, Millburn High School is a safe location with night time security. Also, all students must follow the MLH Code of Conduct.
Do students sleep overnight? Students are encouraged, although not required, to stay overnight as part of the hackathon experience. There will be designated quiet areas for sleeping. If you are uncomfortable staying overnight, you may be picked up by a parent.
When are applications open? Applications are currently open!
What should I bring? Bring your laptop, phone, chargers, a sleeping bag (if desired), excitement, creativity, and a good sense of humor!
How will you decide between applicants? Participants will be selected by a random lottery. Each round of accepted students will have 2-3 days to RSVP.
Do I need to join a team beforehand? Nope, but you can if you want to! We encourage you to meet new students during team formation at beginning of the event. We recommend that you form a team of up to 4 students.
